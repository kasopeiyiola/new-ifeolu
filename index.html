Brief History

Welcome to our school

NEW IFEOLU SCHOOL: The school was founded with the initial name IFE OLU NURSERY AND PRIMARY SCHOOL in the year 1994, the nursery and primary school started on September 8th of that year (1994). The nursery and primary school began with about three teachers and thirty pupils. The school first seeked for her nursery school approval in the year 1996 but due to the duplication of the name Ife - Oluwa nursery and primary school at owode.
The ministry of education advised the school management to either add or remove a word from the school's name for proper distinction.
However, this prompted the school management to therefore conclude on a decision and add the word "NEW' and " UNIQUE" to the school's name which makes it now "NEW - IFE - OLU - UNIQUE - NIP SCHOOL" till date.
On the 26th of September 1996 the school proceeded to get her primary school approval as well. Finally, the school began her college with (5) five students in September, 2012. So far, the school runs both nursery, primary and college and has produced great men and women of good character and reputation in the society.
